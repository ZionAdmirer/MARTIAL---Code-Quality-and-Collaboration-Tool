Activity Timeline

MARTIAL's Activity Timeline feature allows you to visualize the activity timeline of your projects, providing insights into changes over time and trends in development and collaboration. This feature helps you track the progress of your projects and understand how your team is working together.

With Activity Timeline, you can:

  • Visualize commits, pull requests, and issues over a timeline
  • Identify trends in development activity and collaboration
  • Track changes in project milestones and deliverables
  • Analyze team performance and identify areas for improvement

By leveraging MARTIAL's Activity Timeline, you can keep your projects on track, enhance team collaboration, and make data-driven decisions to improve your development processes.
