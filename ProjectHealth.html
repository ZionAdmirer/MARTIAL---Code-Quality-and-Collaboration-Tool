Project Health Metrics

MARTIAL's Project Health Metrics feature provides a comprehensive view of your project's health by analyzing key metrics such as commit frequency and issue resolution rates. With real-time data, you can make informed decisions to ensure the success of your projects.

With Project Health Metrics, you can:

  • Track the number of commits over time to identify activity trends
  • Monitor issue resolution rates to ensure timely bug fixes and feature implementation
  • Visualize project health using intuitive charts and graphs
  • Set custom thresholds and receive alerts for potential issues

By leveraging MARTIAL's Project Health Metrics, you can proactively identify areas for improvement, allocate resources effectively, and maintain a healthy development process throughout your project's lifecycle.
