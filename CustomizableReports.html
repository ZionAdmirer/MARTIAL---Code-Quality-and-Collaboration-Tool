Customizable Reports

MARTIAL's Customizable Reports feature allows you to generate reports tailored to your team's specific needs. Analyze data in formats that work best for your workflow, ensuring that you have the insights necessary to make informed decisions.

With Customizable Reports, you can:

  • Create reports based on various metrics such as code quality, contributor activity, and project health
  • Choose from multiple formats, including PDF, CSV, and HTML
  • Schedule automated reports to be generated and sent to your team
  • Use templates to streamline report creation and maintain consistency

By leveraging MARTIAL's Customizable Reports, you can enhance communication within your team, provide stakeholders with relevant data, and ensure that everyone is aligned with the project's goals.
